About us

We are a closely knitted family of swimmers based in Ilorin, Kwara State. The team is primarily mandated to train students of the University of Ilorin for competitions. Due to our huge impact so far, we have also taken up the added role of training non-students of the University, spanning all age groups. We use standard-sized pools for our training sessions, which hold at Kwara Hotel, G.R.A, Ilorin.
